Integration with external software

Models developed using Koogu may be employed outside of the Koogu framework, by integrating with other standalone software tools. Currently, models that you train using Koogu can be easily incorporated in Raven Pro.

Raven Pro

Raven Pro, starting with v1.6.5, includes support for using Koogu-trained models natively within its user interface — simply load a trained model and apply to audio files you are currently analyzing, and detections will be shown immediately in the waveform/spectrogram panes.

Before you can use your models in Raven Pro, you will need to generate metadata to describe your model’s inputs and outputs.
